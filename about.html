About Us

Ozark the Dog was created to make it easier for people to connect with their state senators and representatives, and hold them accountable. Inspired by Leeja Miller’s video, “Why Aren’t Democrats DOING Anything?”, this tool empowers you to take action and make your voice heard.

Why the name Ozark the Dog? Like Ozark, who barks relentlessly at every stranger at the door, refusing to be ignored, this platform encourages you to make noise and demand action from Congress to help save democracy.

Let’s be clear: Ozark the Dog stands for justice and equality. We support LGBTQ+ rights, reproductive freedom, Black Lives Matter, civil rights, immigrants’ rights, the liberation of Palestine, and fighting fascism and white supremacy.
